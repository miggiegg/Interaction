With integrated audio and video, users not only read content, but also hear and watch. Whether an audio clip of a historic speech or a video timelapse of a plant’s lifecycle, these additions add a whole new level of interactivity to reading and learning.  Audio and video can be embedded for online or offline usage. With custom HTML, large files can be streamed with internet access to minimize file space. Other audio and video with smaller file sizes can be implemented right within the pages for offline viewing.  Vanadium Press sources audio and video that are in the public domain or are royalty-free to save costs associated with recording or filming new material. With projects with specialized audio and video needs, we partner with other content creators to deliver high-quality content.
