With integrated audio and video, users not only read content, but also hear and watch it. Whether an audio clip of a historic speech or a video timelapse of a plant’s lifecycle, these effects add a new dimension to reading and learning.  Audio and video can be embedded for online or offline use. With custom HTML, large files can be streamed through the internet to minimize file space on devices. Smaller audio and video files can be implemented right within the pages for offline viewing.  We source audio and video that are in the public domain or are royalty-free to save on costs associated with recording or filming new material. For projects that have specialized audio and video, we partner with other content creators to deliver high-quality content.
